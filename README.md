### 🚨 Common Financial Mistakes to Avoid

Many people unknowingly make decisions that impact their long-term financial success.

Avoid these common mistakes:

❌ Delaying retirement planning  
❌ Investing without a structured strategy  
❌ Ignoring life and income protection  
❌ Not planning for tax and estate implications  
❌ Relying only on employer pension funds  

The earlier these are addressed, the stronger your financial future becomes.

---

### ✅ Questions You Should Ask a Financial Planner

Before choosing a financial advisor, ask the right questions to ensure you receive the right guidance.

1. **Are you an independent financial advisor?**  
2. **Do you provide full financial planning or just products?**  
3. **How do you approach retirement planning?**  
4. **Do you assist with tax and estate planning?**  
5. **Will my financial plan adapt as my life changes?**

A good financial planner will always prioritize clarity and transparency.
